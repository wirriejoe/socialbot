# Central America Founders Retreat Itinerary

## Executive Summary

This report synthesizes research from **700+ Instagram posts** across founders retreats, entrepreneur travel, and Central America destinations to create a comprehensive 1-2 week itinerary for a founders retreat.

**Key Finding:** Guatemala (Lake Atitlan) + Costa Rica combination offers the optimal balance of affordability, transformational experiences, and adventure activities.

---

## Recommended Route

**Guatemala → El Salvador → Nicaragua → Costa Rica**

---

## Option A: 1-Week Intensive

### Days 1-3: Guatemala - Lake Atitlan

**Base:** San Marcos La Laguna or Santa Cruz

#### Accommodations

| Venue | Description | Source |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Eagles Nest Atitlan | Retreat venue with volcano views, yoga decks, wellness programming | [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/p/DFq4tBtuCAv/) |
| Casa Prana Atitlan | Boutique retreat on the shores, peaceful energy | [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/p/DNd1yM0Rm-q/) |
| Lakeview Lodge | Luxury Airbnb with sunrise volcano views | [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/p/DGhbWwCuI0E/) |
| La Casa del Mundo | Iconic lakefront hotel (1M+ views) | [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/p/DE91AKzRrH_/) |

#### Activities

- Morning yoga/meditation sessions overlooking the lake
- Founder workshop sessions (strategy, vision, vulnerability exercises)
- Salsa dancing class - excellent team bonding
- Kayaking/paddleboarding on the lake
- Visit Mayan villages (San Juan, Santiago)
- Sound healing experiences at wellness centers

#### Why It Works for Founders

Peaceful, affordable, strong wellness infrastructure, no cell signal in some villages = forced digital detox

---

### Days 4-5: Guatemala - Antigua & Volcano Acatenango

#### Experience

- **Volcano Acatenango overnight hike** - Watch Volcan Fuego erupt at night ([Source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DMVqX7hstYN/))
- Colonial town exploration in Antigua
- Coffee farm tours (team building + caffeine supply)

#### Accommodations

- Boutique hotels around Antigua's central square
- El Convento for luxury option

#### Why It Works for Founders

Epic shared challenge (the hike), bonding through adversity, incredible photo opportunities for team content

---

### Days 6-7: Costa Rica - Arenal/La Fortuna

#### Activities

- **Hot springs** - Tabacon or Baldi for recovery/networking ([Source](https://www.instagram.com/p/CuU6DEDtOvT/))
- **White water rafting** - highly recommended team activity ([Source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DMVqX7hstYN/))
- Zip-lining canopy tours
- Hanging bridges in cloud forest
- Canyoneering/waterfall rappelling

#### Accommodations

- Nayara Springs (luxury)
- Arenal Observatory Lodge
- Tabacon Resort

#### Why It Works for Founders

Adrenaline activities build trust, hot springs provide recovery/reflection time

## Programming Elements

Based on successful founder retreats including [@flyingwithfounders](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTRYZ3njUHp/):

### Daily Structure

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning | Yoga/movement + healthy breakfast |
| Mid-morning | Workshop content (strategy, fundraising, personal growth) |
| Afternoon | Adventure activity or free exploration |
| Evening | Group dinner + facilitated conversation or free time |

### Workshop Topics That Work

- Vulnerability and authentic leadership
- Vision casting and goal setting
- Healthy founder habits / burnout prevention
- Mastermind sessions (problem-solving circles)
- Exit planning / "healthy exits"

### Team Building Activities (Ranked by Impact)

1. **Volcano overnight hike (Acatenango)** - highest impact
2. **White water rafting**
3. **Surf lessons** - failure + learning together
4. **Salsa/dance class** - outside comfort zone
5. **Hot springs** - natural networking environment

## Budget Considerations

| Country | Budget | Mid-Range | Luxury |
|---------|--------|-----------|--------|
| Guatemala | $50-80/night | $100-200/night | $250+/night |
| El Salvador | $40-70/night | $80-150/night | $200+/night |
| Nicaragua | $50-100/night | $120-250/night | $400+/night |

> **Note:** Guatemala is notably "very affordable" and "less touristy" per multiple sources.

## Logistics

### Flights

- **Fly into:** Guatemala City (GUA)
- **Fly out of:** San Jose (SJO) or Liberia (LIR), Costa Rica

### Internal Transport

Private drivers recommended for groups (arrange through hotels)

### Best Time to Visit

November - April (dry season)

### Ideal Group Size

8-15 founders for intimacy + diverse perspectives

## Recommendation

**For Maximum Impact:** Start with Guatemala (Lake Atitlan + Acatenango volcano) for the first half - it offers the best cost-effectiveness with the highest "transformational" factor. Finish in Costa Rica (Nosara or Arenal) for polish and adventure activities.

The combination of:
- **Vulnerability-focused workshops** (mornings)
- **Shared physical challenges** (afternoons)
- **Natural beauty and forced disconnection** (environment)

Creates the optimal conditions for founder bonding, strategic thinking, and personal growth.
